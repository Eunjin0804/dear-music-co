Principles & Laws

OUR PHILOSOPHY

DEAR MUSIC_co is not a factory.
It is a space of creation, protection, and truth.

We believe that art is born from emotion, not pressure.
That identity is not something to be erased, but something to be refined.
That growth should never come from fear.

We do not create idols.
We accompany artists.

CORE PRINCIPLES

1. Emotional Honesty

At DEAR MUSIC_co, emotions are not weaknesses.
They are the foundation of music, performance, and storytelling.

Artists are encouraged to express vulnerability, sensitivity, and personal narratives ‚Äî without being forced to expose what they are not ready to share.

2. Artistic Freedom

We respect individuality.

Each artist keeps their personality, their voice, their style, and their pace.
Concepts are built with artists, not imposed on them.

No member is asked to abandon who they are to fit an image.

3. Protection First

Artists come before content.

Physical health, mental well-being, and personal safety are non-negotiable.
Any form of harassment, humiliation, manipulation, or abuse is strictly forbidden.

Respect is the base of all relationships within the agency.

4. Growth, Not Perfection

We do not expect perfection.
We value effort, sincerity, and evolution.

Mistakes are part of the process.
Learning is encouraged.
Comparison is discouraged.
