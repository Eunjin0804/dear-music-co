BLOOD IN BLOOM

NEXT DEAR — First Album

Blood in Bloom is the first chapter of NEXT DEAR. An album born from pain, growth, and emotional truth.

Nothing is hidden here. Wounds are visible. Fragility becomes strength.

NEXT DEAR does not seek perfection. They exist in the in-between — between shadow and light, between falling and becoming.

IDENTITY

In this album, NEXT DEAR embodies a generation learning to bloom with blood still on their hands.

Each member represents a stage of healing — fear, anger, loneliness, acceptance, and awakening.

Violet becomes their color: transformation, inner night, and silent rebirth.

Blood in Bloom is not a performance. It is a collective confession.

TITLE TRACK — BLOOD IN BLOOM

The title song captures the moment when pain stops being weakness.

It speaks of open scars, self-love learned too late, and the courage to continue even when everything feels broken.

The chorus is a promise: even wounded, we can still bloom.

Sonically, the track blends a dark, suffocating atmosphere with a slow emotional rise — like a flower growing through concrete.

MESSAGE

Blood in Bloom is dedicated to those who grew up too fast.

To those who learned how to survive before learning how to dream.

This is not an album about pain. It is an album about what is born after.
